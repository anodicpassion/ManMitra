How does the AI companion adapt to my personality? expand_more

The AI companion learns from your interactions, adjusting its communication style and responses to better match your preferences and personality over time.

What is sentiment analysis and how does it work? expand_more

Sentiment analysis allows the AI to understand the emotional tone behind your words. It analyzes your text to identify feelings like joy, sadness, or frustration, helping it to respond with more empathy and support.

How are the AI's responses generated? expand_more

Our AI uses a large language model trained on a vast and diverse dataset of text. This allows it to generate human-like, relevant, and supportive responses to your thoughts and feelings.

What are the ethical considerations for using the AI companion? expand_more

We prioritize your privacy and data security. All conversations are encrypted and anonymized. Our AI is designed to be a supportive tool, not a replacement for professional help. We have strict guidelines to prevent harmful or biased responses.
